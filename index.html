Niles Rivers is a multi-talented performer, songwriter, and teacher whose voice has moved strangers to tears and whose presence lights up every stage he stands on. From starring as Simba in Disney’s *The Lion King* to collaborating with legendary producer David Foster, Niles brings decades of storytelling through sound, rhythm, and character.
