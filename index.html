Journal: Behind the Song

“The Longing” is about the sorrow in His love—not the kind that’s angry or impatient, but the ache of wanting to give us everything, and watching us doubt it.

He died to give us fullness. To pour out mercy, identity, healing, and nearness. But so often, we hold back from receiving it—not because we’re proud, but because we quietly don’t believe He could really love us that much.

This song came from a dream, but it carries something that feels bigger than me. If it reaches you, I pray it softens the places that have forgotten they’re loved.

If it speaks to you, receive it freely—or give what you feel led to.
